Course Loops:

  • Hero Loop - 19 Holes (Red/Rec)
  • Superhero Loop - 32 Holes (White/Intermediate)
  • Ultra Hero Loop - 33 Holes (Blue/Pro)
  • Freaky Hero Loop... to Parts Unknown... Possibly 44 Holes

Heroes Disc Golf Course is a professional caliber disc golf course located in northern Connecticut. We are open all year round and will always be in playing condition regardless of the weather. Make a plan to come out for a day to get a great experience!

The main 32 hole course has four configurations and is designed to both remember our sport's par 3 disc golf history and embrace the longer, more challenging modern courses being designed and built today. Heroes Disc Golf has an ideal combination of courses.

  • Hero Loop: Shorter holes that avoid throwing over water, making it a perfect family, beginner, or casual round with a friendly challenge.
  • Superhero Loop: It will test your skills, but not beat you up too much. The loop incorporates a couple water holes to test your nerves.
  • Ultra Hero Loop: The technical and tight lines of play, combined with thrilling distance and water shots, make the Ultra Hero Loop a demanding and inspiring course for seasoned players. Bring your A-Game.
  • Freaky Hero Loop: ... To parts unknown ...

Come and enjoy disc golf as it was meant to be.
