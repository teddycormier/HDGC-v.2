TEE TIME INFORMATION

Remember:

  • If you are more than 10 minutes late, we cannot guarantee you will be able to play.

  • Tee Times can be booked 32 days in advance
  • Tee Times are required and must be prepaid

Members:

  • Please make separate bookings for each member
  • Please do not include any non-members in your bookings
  • Make a separate booking for any non-members
